  • Guidance for Healthcare Facilities

    Healthcare facilities need to provide proper care for all patients in every safest possible way for patients and healthcare personnel and at the suitable level, whether patients need care at home, at hospital, urgent care, emergency room care, inpatient care, or intensive care. This guidance outlines goals and strategies for healthcare facilities to operate effectively and safely during the COVID-19 pandemic to every kind of patient. These guidelines bring that shared knowledge to the world to achieve these key objectives:

    • Operate effectively all patients during the COVID-19 pandemic
    • Maintain public confidence in the facilities which comply with these guidelines
    • Reduce the need to provide in-person care by changing the way to deliver services
    • Provide necessary in-person clinical services for conditions other than COVID-19 in the safest way possible, minimizing disease transmission to patients, HCP, and others
    • Consider the wellbeing, safety, privacy of patients, staff and visitors
    • Follow-up with patients after they are discharged from the hospital.
    • Follow infection prevention and control recommendations made to their setting

    These Guidelines will be continually reviewed, updated and expanded according to situation
